Who are we and what do we do?

Teufel is a German brand that designs and sells
- smart loudspeakers,
- bluetooth speakers,
- headphones,
- TV soundbars and
- home cinema devices.

Our focus is consumer audio products, and increasingly companion apps for those products.

Apps that allow, among other things,
- to browse online music services such as
    - TIDAL,
    - Napster,
    - Tune In,
    - Soundcloud,
- perform perform over-the-air firmware upgrades,
- device setup and configuration,
- multi-room grouping,
- equalisation,
- volume control,
- transport controls,
- and management of
    - favourites,
    - playlists,
    - timers and
    - alarm clock.
